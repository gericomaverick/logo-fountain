# Product Brief: Logo Fountain

**Date:** 2026-02-24  
**Author:** Product Owner Agent

## Vision
Logo Fountain is a premium UK-based logo design service that delivers bespoke, human-designed logos through a structured, auditable digital workflow.

## Problem Statement
Clients often struggle with the chaotic nature of informal email-based logo design processes, leading to potential miscommunications and dissatisfaction with the final product.

## Target Users

### Persona 1: Client
- **Role:** Business owner or entrepreneur  
- **Pain Points:** Inconsistent quality, lack of communication, and difficulty in providing feedback.  
- **Goals:** Obtain a high-quality logo that represents their brand effectively.

### Persona 2: Admin/Designer
- **Role:** Logo project administrator or designer  
- **Pain Points:** Overwhelmed by managing multiple client requests and revisions.  
- **Goals:** Streamline workflow and ensure timely delivery of projects.

## Value Proposition
For business owners who struggle with disorganized logo design processes, Logo Fountain is a managed logo design service that guarantees high quality and efficient delivery. Unlike traditional email-based services, our product utilizes a structured platform that emphasizes transparency and communication.

## MVP Features

### Must-Have
1. Secure login — Essential for user authentication and data protection.  
2. Project management dashboard — To track project statuses and manage client communications.  

### Should-Have
1. Feedback and revision submission feature — Allows clients to submit structured feedback efficiently.  

### Nice-to-Have (Post-MVP)
1. Advanced analytics dashboard — Provide insights into user satisfaction and project performance.

## Success Metrics
| Metric | Target | Rationale |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| 50-100 paid projects | Within first 3 months | Measure initial market acceptance. |
| < 2 hours admin time/project | Efficiency goal | Reduce operational overhead. |
| ≥ 85% completion rate | Quality assurance | Ensure prompt project completions. |
| ≥ 50 Net Promoter Score | Customer satisfaction | Gauge overall user happiness. |

## Constraints
- Monolithic architecture with a focus on reliability.  
- Solo/lean development team.
