# Product Brief: Logo Fountain

## Overview
Logo Fountain is a premium UK-based logo design service, providing high-quality logos that cater to the high-end small business market.

### Target Users:
- Clients purchasing logo packages.
- Admins/designers managing project delivery.

### MVP Feature List:
- Marketing site.
- Client login.
- Project dashboard.
- Concept upload and feedback loop.
- Final asset delivery.

### Success Metrics:
- Clients complete projects without manual email chains.
- Admin workload reduced by >50%.
- High perceived service quality.
- Repeat customers and referrals.

### Constraints:
- Solo/lean development team.
- Long-term maintainability required.
- Prioritize reliability over experimental features.
