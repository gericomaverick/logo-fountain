## 1. Vision

Build a reliable, repeatable system for delivering bespoke, professionally designed logos to UK small businesses, replacing ad-hoc email coordination with a structured, high-quality digital workflow.

Logo Fountain positions itself as a premium, high-trust service — not a template marketplace.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Small businesses struggle to obtain professional branding due to:

- Fragmented communication with designers
- Unclear revision processes
- Lack of transparency on progress
- Manual asset delivery
- Inconsistent service quality

Current solutions are either low-quality template tools or expensive agencies with slow turnaround.

---

## 3. Target Users

### 3.1 Primary: Small Business Owners
- Limited branding expertise
- Value reliability and speed
- Will pay for quality

### 3.2 Secondary: Start-up Founders
- Need rapid brand identity
- Limited internal design capacity
- Require multiple iterations

### 3.3 Internal: Admin / Designers
- Manage multiple concurrent projects
- Need structured workflows
- Require auditability

---

## 4. Value Proposition

Logo Fountain delivers bespoke UK-designed logos through a transparent, managed workflow with predictable pricing, revision control, and professional asset delivery.

Unlike generic logo generators, clients receive human-designed work with accountability and quality assurance.

---

## 5. Business Model

- Fixed-price logo packages
- Tiered by:
  - Number of concepts
  - Number of revisions
  - Delivery speed
- Optional upsells:
  - Extra revisions
  - Expedited delivery
  - Brand kits
- Payment collected upfront via Stripe
- No subscriptions in Phase 1

---

## 6. Core User Workflow

1. Client purchases package
2. Creates account
3. Submits structured brief
4. Designer reviews brief
5. Concepts uploaded
6. Client reviews and comments
7. Revision cycles occur
8. Final approval
9. Assets delivered
10. Project archived

---

## 7. Admin Workflow

- Review incoming briefs
- Assign/manage designers
- Upload concepts
- Upload revisions
- Override entitlement limits
- Respond to feedback
- Issue refunds
- Archive projects
- View audit timeline
- Track package usage

---

## 11. Key Assumptions

- Clients will use dashboard instead of email
- Designers can adapt to workflow
- Most projects complete in ≤3 revisions
- Email + in-app notifications suffice

---

## 12. Delivery Constraints

- Solo founder
- Limited contractors
- Managed services only
- Minimal DevOps
- Avoid microservices

---

## 13. Risks

- Clients bypassing system via email
- Scope creep via revisions
- Payment disputes
- Designer availability
