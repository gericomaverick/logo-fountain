# Product Brief: Logo Fountain

**Date:** 2026-02-24  
**Author:** Product Owner Agent

## Vision  
Logo Fountain is a premium UK-based logo design service aiming to simplifies the logo acquisition process for small businesses and startups, providing a seamless management experience for clients and designers alike.  

## Problem Statement  
Many small businesses struggle with establishing their brand identity due to inadequate design resources, often resorting to generic solutions that don’t reflect their unique identity. Logo Fountain aims to provide a tailored solution that meets the specific needs of these businesses.  

## Target Users  
### Persona 1: Small Business Owner  
- **Role:** Entrepreneur / Business Owner  
- **Pain Points:** Lack of design knowledge, inability to effectively communicate design needs.  
- **Goals:** Establish a strong brand identity, ensure professional appearance.

### Persona 2: Admin / Designer  
- **Role:** Design Manager  
- **Pain Points:** Managing client expectations, delivering quality designs on tight timelines.  
- **Goals:** Deliver high-quality designs efficiently, ensure client satisfaction.  

## Value Proposition  
For small business owners who struggle with establishing a brand identity, Logo Fountain is a premium logo design service that provides tailored, high-quality design solutions. Unlike generic logo generators, our product offers bespoke designs created by experienced UK designers.  

## MVP Features  
### Must-Have  
1. Secure client login — Essential for project management.  
2. Concept upload and feedback loop — Allows clients to provide input.  
3. Final asset delivery — Ensures clients receive their completed logos.  

### Should-Have  
1. Project dashboard — Improves user experience by centralizing project information.  
2. Revision requests — Facilitates better communication between clients and designers.  

### Nice-to-Have (Post-MVP)  
1. Advanced analytics — Enables better understanding of client interactions.  
2. Automated workflows — Streamlines the design process further.  

## Success Metrics  
| Metric | Target | Rationale |  
|--------|--------|-----------|  
| User acquisition | 200 clients in the first 6 months | Indicates market fit and demand |  
| Client satisfaction rate | 90% positive feedback | Measures service quality |  
| Repeat projects | 20% of clients return for additional services | Indicates trust and reliability |  

## Constraints  
- Solo/lean development team  
- Must be maintainable long-term  
- Prioritize reliability over experimental features
