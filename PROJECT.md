# Logo Fountain

## One-line summary
A logo design website producing high quality logos in the UK. The service will offer a tailored end-to-end experience with a client dashboard where they recieve their concepts and can have revisions, feedback and their final files. Me as the admin will be able to login to my own dashboard as admin and upload concepts, revisions to concepts, reply to feedback and see project statuses. 

## Target users
- Startups or small-medium sizes businesses looking to have a professional, high quality logo designed that isn't AI generated.
- Designer/admin managing delivery

## What success looks like
- Clients can log in, see concepts, give feedback which triggers a revision, approve/reject
- Clear project progress visibility
- Ability to view concepts and their revisions. 
- Simple admin workflow

## Constraints / preferences
- Human, friendly tone in copy (no AI-feel)
- Prefer structured, reviewable artifacts in _bmad-output
